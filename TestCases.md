**Pre-requisites**:

- Products are available for purchase.

**Expected Result**:
- The basket count should increase each time a product is added.
- The count should match the number of items added.

**Actual Result**:
- The basket count increases each time a product is added.
- The count matches the number of items added.

**Expected Result**:

- Selecting "Collect (FREE)" should not add any additional charges to the total price.

**Actual Result**:
- The product details (name, price, quantity) are displayed correctly.
- Selecting "Collect (FREE)" did not add any additional charges to the total price.
